## The Current Phase

We are in **pre-sales strategy mode**. The product is not yet on the App Store and has no paying customers. The job right now is:

1. Build the materials (this folder)
2. Validate the pitch with Drew and 2-3 friendly brewery owners (no-pressure conversations)
3. Identify the 10 breweries we'll approach the week the app hits TestFlight
4. Close the first brewery on a **free trial → paid** conversion path

We do not cold-pitch until the App Store listing is live and the onboarding flow is smooth. Selling a product that isn't downloadable yet is the wrong move.

## The Goal

- **First paid brewery:** Week of TestFlight launch
- **5 paid breweries:** 30 days post-launch
- **50 paid breweries:** 90 days post-launch
- **500 paid breweries:** 6 months post-launch ($75K MRR)
