## The current phase

Pre-sales strategy. The product has 2128 passing tests, the onboarding flow is smooth, the claim funnel is production-ready ([Sprint 145](../history/retros/sprint-145-retro.md)), and the demo is tight. What's left is the **go signal** — TestFlight live, legal entity formed, first warm intro pulled.

The job right now:

1. Build the materials (this folder — largely done)
2. Validate the pitch with [Drew](../../.claude/agents/drew.md) and 2-3 friendly brewery owners (no-pressure)
3. Identify the 10 breweries we'll approach the week TestFlight lands ([target-breweries.md](target-breweries.md))
4. Close the first brewery on a free-trial → paid conversion path

We do **not** cold-pitch until the App Store listing is live. See [design/app-store-metadata.md](../design/app-store-metadata.md).

## The goal

- **First paid brewery:** week of TestFlight launch
- **5 paid breweries:** 30 days post-launch
- **50 paid breweries:** 90 days post-launch
