## Research Methodology

When identifying new targets, look for:

1. **Instagram** — Search `#craftbeer[city]`, look for taprooms with 1K–20K followers, active posting, and visible paper punch cards in photos or stories
2. **Google Maps** — "Craft brewery [city]" → filter by independent (not chain), 4.0+ stars, 200+ reviews
3. **Untappd** — Look for breweries with 50–500 check-ins/month. Enough traction to care, small enough to still feel the pain
4. **Local craft beer subreddits** — Owner activity in communities like r/asheville, r/Denver, r/portlandbeer signals openness to community engagement
5. **Drew's network** — Always the highest-quality leads. Ask Drew for 2 new intros per month.

## Notes on Timing

**Don't pitch:**
- Before the App Store listing is live
- On a Friday afternoon or Saturday (bar shift)
- During a major local beer festival week (they're slammed)

**Do pitch:**
- Tuesday–Thursday, mid-morning
- Right after a new feature ships that's relevant to them (events, board, loyalty)
- When Drew can make a warm intro in person
