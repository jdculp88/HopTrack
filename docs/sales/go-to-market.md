# HopTrack — Go-To-Market Strategy
**Owner:** Taylor · **Updated:** 2026-03-27

---

## The Big Picture

HopTrack replaces paper punch cards and disconnected spreadsheets with a platform that brewery owners actually love. The consumer app and the brewery dashboard are two sides of the same coin — one doesn't work without the other.

**The flywheel:**
> Brewery joins → customers download app to get stamps → customers check in + share → their friends discover the brewery → more customers → more brewery value → more breweries join

The first 10 breweries are the hardest. After that, the consumer side starts pulling.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Now → First 5 Paid Breweries)

**Goal:** Prove the model. Get 5 paid breweries and 500 consumer app users in the same markets.

**How:**
- Warm intros through Drew's network (Asheville first)
- Free trial → paid conversion (14 days, no credit card)
- White-glove onboarding: Taylor handles setup personally for the first 5
- No cold outreach at scale yet — every brewery gets a human conversation

**Success metrics:**
- 5 paid breweries
- 500 consumer MAU
- 1 case study with real data ("X regulars using stamp cards, Y% repeat visit rate")
- Net Promoter Score from first 5 breweries: target 8+/10

**What we're NOT doing yet:**
- Paid advertising
- Conference sponsorships
- Cold email at scale
- Anything that requires case studies we don't have

---

## Phase 2 — Traction (5 → 50 Paid Breweries)

**Goal:** Establish a repeatable sales motion. Each close takes less than 2 hours of Taylor's time.

**How:**
- Case studies from Phase 1 become the primary sales tool
- Referral program: breweries refer other breweries for a free month
- Drew actively attends 2 craft beer industry events/quarter and demos The Board live
- `/for-breweries` page updated with real testimonials and data
- Start targeting brewery associations and Facebook groups for organic reach

**Hire trigger:** When Taylor is closing 3+ breweries per week, hire Customer Success #1

**Success metrics:**
- 50 paid breweries
- $2,450–$7,450 MRR (mix of Tap + Cask)
- Customer Success hire made
- Referral program live

---

## Phase 3 — Scale (50 → 500 Paid Breweries)

**Goal:** Reach $75K MRR with a repeatable, partially automated sales + onboarding process.

**How:**
- Growth/SEO hire drives inbound (brewery owners searching "digital loyalty program for brewery")
- Analytics Engineer hire enables data-driven case studies
- Self-serve onboarding reduces Taylor's time per close to < 30 min
- Barrel tier (custom) conversations started with multi-location operators
- Conference presence: GABF, CBC, regional craft beer associations

**Success metrics:**
- 500 paid breweries
- $75K MRR
- 3-person revenue team
- Self-serve onboarding completion rate > 70%

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness | Our Edge |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Untappd for Business** | Brand recognition, consumer base | Not a loyalty tool, no dashboard | Full-stack: loyalty + analytics + tap list + events |
| **Square Loyalty** | Distribution, easy setup | Generic, not brewery-specific, no consumer app | Built for craft beer culture |
| **Loyaltylion / Stamp Me** | Polished loyalty UX | No brewery context, no tap list, no TV display | The Board. Drew's P0 list. The whole thing. |
| **Paper punch cards** | Zero cost, familiar | No data, no analytics, lost cards, no social | Everything HopTrack does |
| **Spreadsheets** | Free, flexible | Manual, no consumer-facing, no loyalty | Automated, beautiful, social |

**Our moat:** HopTrack is the only platform that combines consumer social (check-ins, achievements, friends feed) with brewery operations (tap list, loyalty, analytics, events). Nobody else does both.
