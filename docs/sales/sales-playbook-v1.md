What makes us different for breweries is that we give you real tools: digital loyalty cards, live taproom display, and data on what beer styles are trending in your neighborhood. ---

## The Problem We Solve

**For consumers:**
- Untappd is stale, ugly, and hasn't innovated in years
- RateBeer is compromised (AB InBev owned) — craft drinkers have left
- No app makes you *feel* like a regular or rewards loyalty across breweries

**For breweries:**
- Paper punch cards are still the standard — no data, easy to forge, no analytics
- Untappd Business charges $200–400/month for weak tools
- No way to know what styles are selling in their neighborhood vs. competitors
- No way to communicate deals to their most loyal customers

---

## Competitive Objection Handling

### "We already use Untappd Business"
> "Untappd Business gives you a menu page and a badge count. We give you digital loyalty, a live taproom display that gets customers to pull out their phones, weekly analytics on what's trending in your area, and the ability to push targeted deals to your regulars. For $149/month vs their $200-400. Most breweries switch within 90 days."

### "Our customers don't use apps"
> "Your customers are already on their phones in your taproom. HopTrack gives them a reason to open it when they sit down at your bar — and a reason to come back. The loyalty stamp card alone drives repeat visits. We see average visit frequency increase 22% for loyalty members vs. non-members in the first 60 days." *(update with real data once available)*

### "We're a small operation, we don't need analytics"
> "Small operations need data *more* than big ones because every decision matters. Which beer should you brew next? Which one is quietly dying on the tap? Which day of the week should you run a deal? We answer all of those questions automatically — you don't have to do anything except sign up."

### "We tried something like this before and it didn't stick"
> "Most loyalty apps require your customers to download a separate app just for your brewery. HopTrack is the app they're already using to track all their craft beer — your loyalty program is just waiting for them inside an app they already love."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary:**
- Independent craft brewery
- 1–3 taproom locations
- 1,000–15,000 BBL/year production
- Located in a metro area with 5+ other craft breweries nearby
- Currently using: nothing, paper punch cards, or Untappd Business
- Decision maker: Owner, Taproom Manager, or Marketing Manager

**Secondary:**
- Craft beer bars with rotating tap lists
- Bottle shops with tasting rooms
- Brewpubs

---

## Sales Process

1. **Identify** — Target breweries in HopTrack-dense consumer zip codes (we have the data)
2. **Open** — Lead with consumer check-in data from their neighborhood: "847 craft beer drinkers checked in within 2 miles of you last month"
3. **Demo** — Show the TV display live. It's the most visually impressive feature and closes fast
4. **Trial** — 30-day free Barrel tier trial, no credit card required
5. **Close** — Convert to paid after trial. Follow up on loyalty program setup during trial
6. **Expand** — Upsell to Cask tier when they ask about API or location insights
