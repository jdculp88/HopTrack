## Demo Flow (15 minutes)

1. **Open with the demo dashboard** (hoptrack.beer/demo/dashboard) — 3 min
   - "This is what your dashboard would look like with real data"
   - Point out: today's visits, top beers, ROI card, activity feed

2. **Show The Board** — 2 min
   - Full-screen tap list display
   - "This runs on any TV or tablet. Updates when you change a beer."

3. **Show the consumer app** — 3 min
   - Check-in flow, stamp card, brewery page
   - "Your customers earn rewards just by visiting"

4. **Show the one-pager** (hoptrack.beer/for-breweries/one-pager) — 1 min
   - "You can print this as a table tent. Customers scan the QR to start checking in."

5. **Pricing** — 2 min
   - "$49/month for Tap tier. 14-day free trial. No credit card."
   - "Annual saves you 2 months."

6. **Ask** — 2 min
   - "Want to claim your listing and try it for 14 days?"
   - Walk them through the claim flow if they say yes
