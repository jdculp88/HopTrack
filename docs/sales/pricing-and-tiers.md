## The Tiers

### 🍺 Tap — $49/month
*The entry point. For taprooms just getting started with digital.*

**Includes:**
- Brewery dashboard (tap list management, drag reorder, 86'd toggle)
- "The Board" — live TV tap list display
- Brewery profile on consumer app (explore + check-ins)
- Upcoming events (create, manage, publish)
- Basic analytics (visits, unique visitors, avg rating, peak times)
- Loyalty program (1 active stamp card)

**Best for:** Single-location taproom, owner-operated, 500–1,500 monthly visitors, just replacing paper punch cards

---

### 🪣 Cask — $149/month
*The full platform. For breweries serious about their regulars.*

**Everything in Tap, plus:**
- Full loyalty dashboard (stamp progress bars, top customers, redemption history)
- Advanced analytics (Top Beers by Rating, Repeat Visitor %, full chart suite)
- Multiple loyalty programs
- Promotions (happy hour, BOGO, percent off)
- Priority support

**Best for:** Brewpub or active taproom, 1,500–5,000 monthly visitors, loyalty program as a core retention tool

---

### 🛢️ Barrel — Custom
*For operators who need more.*

**Everything in Cask, plus:**
- Multi-location support
- White-label options
- Custom onboarding + dedicated account manager
- SLA + uptime guarantee
- API access (for POS integrations)
- Custom reporting

**Best for:** Regional breweries, multi-tap operations, anyone who says "we need to talk to our team"

## Annual Billing

Always offer annual. Two months free.

| Tier | Monthly | Annual | Savings |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tap | $49/mo | $490/yr | $98 |
| Cask | $149/mo | $1,490/yr | $298 |

Lead with monthly in the pitch. Offer annual at close if they're hesitant on cost.

## The 14-Day Free Trial

- No credit card required
- All features unlocked (Cask-level for the trial — let them feel the full product)
- Taylor personally checks in at day 5 and day 12
- Day 12 call: "What's been useful? Ready to keep it going?"
- At day 14, downgrade to free (tap list only, no loyalty) if they don't convert — keeps them in the ecosystem

---

## Discount Policy

**What we offer:**
- Annual billing: 2 months free (documented above)
- Referral program: 1 free month per brewery referred that converts to paid
- **That's it.**

**What we don't do:**
- Monthly discounts ("can we do $35/month?") → No. Hold the price.
- Free for "exposure" → No.
- Extended free trials beyond 14 days → No, except in rare cases with Morgan approval.

**Why:** The price is already low for the value. Discounting trains customers to negotiate and devalues the product. If they can't justify $49/month after a 14-day trial, the problem is either the product fit or the pitch — not the price.

## Upgrade Triggers (Tap → Cask)

Watch for these signals in check-in calls — they're natural Cask upsell moments:

- "I wish I could see which customers are close to their reward"
- "Can I run a happy hour special in the app?"
- "The analytics are good but I want to see more"
- "Can I run two loyalty programs for different nights?"
- "Our staff keeps asking who our top regulars are"

When you hear any of these: "That's actually a Cask feature — want me to switch you over? It's $100 more a month and I think you'd get that back in one extra regular visit per week."
