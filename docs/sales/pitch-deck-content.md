## Appendix — Objection Handling

**"We already use Untappd."**
"Untappd is great for beer ratings. HopTrack is your loyalty program, analytics dashboard, and live tap list display in one. They complement each other — your customers can use both."

**"Our customers won't download another app."**
"HopTrack is a web app — no download required. Works in any browser. But we also support PWA install for customers who want the app-like experience."

**"We don't have time to set this up."**
"30 minutes. Upload your logo, add your beers, turn on loyalty. We handle the rest. And our Customer Success team will check in on Day 3 and Day 7 to make sure you're set."

**"$49/month is a lot for a small brewery."**
"One loyalty redemption bringing back a regular who spends $30 pays for the month. Most breweries see ROI within the first week of their trial."

**"What if we cancel?"**
"Cancel anytime. No contracts, no penalties. Your data stays safe and you can reactivate whenever you want."
