## Description
HopTrack is the craft beer companion that turns every brewery visit into a journey.

Check in beers, rate your pours, earn XP, and unlock achievements as you explore the craft beer world. Track your sessions at breweries or at home, build your beer passport, and share your tasting notes with friends.

KEY FEATURES:
- Session-based check-ins — log multiple beers in a single brewery visit
- Beer passport — track every style you've tried
- XP & achievements — earn badges for exploring new styles and breweries
- Friends feed — see what your crew is drinking
- Beer of the Week — discover featured brews
- Brewery explorer — find new spots with map and list views
- Share cards — beautiful session recaps to share on social
- Push notifications — stay in the loop when friends check in

FOR BREWERY OWNERS:
HopTrack also offers a brewery dashboard with tap list management, digital loyalty programs, and real analytics. Visit hoptrack.app/for-breweries to learn more.

Built with love by beer people, for beer people. Drink responsibly.
